Steve Blum

One of the best Dub Voice Actors

Timeline of Steve Blums life

Born April 29 1960 in Santa Monica California

Began is VA carrer in 1984

First big Voice role was in 1998 on Cowboy Bebop as Spike Spegail

In 2000 Cartoon Network Hired him to do the voice for TOM the host of Toonami and he still plays the role to this day (2021)

On June 5 2012 He was awarded Guinness World Record for being the most prolific video game voice actor.

He Married Voice actress Elizabeth McGlynn in 2017

He has 3 sons from a previous relationship one is a VA one is a teacher
